===========================
dirac-wms-job-status
===========================

  Retrieve status of the given DIRAC job

Usage::

  dirac-wms-job-status [option|cfgfile] ... JobID ...

Arguments::

  JobID:    DIRAC Job ID 

Example::

  $ dirac-wms-job-status 2
  JobID=2 Status=Done; MinorStatus=Execution Complete; Site=EELA.UTFSM.cl;
